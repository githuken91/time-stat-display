# Time Stat Display

A simple mod that displays the time of day in the bottom-left corner.

## Why use this mod?

Although there may be other mods doing similar things, this mod is very simple and fulfills its designed purpose. Being a superior version of the ingame clock item. The clock item only tells you if it's day or night, whereas, with this mod, you know the time of day down to 30 minute intervals.
